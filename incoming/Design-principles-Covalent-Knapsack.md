# Covalent / Design principles

The design principles developed by the Platform Design team are meant to guide the thinking, designing, and building of all of the Teradata products. The principles articulate the fundamental goals all design decisions can be measured against externally as well as internally.

### 

Be easy

Simplify content and interactions and enable collaboration between AI and humans by focusing on usability and guiding users through the easiest paths to get a job done. 

### 

Be scalable

Gain leverage by designing cohesive interfaces that can grow with our uses, within our ecosystem, and with our industry. There should be no isolated features, functionality, or experiences.

### 

Be trustworthy

Promote clarity, inclusivity, and accessibility to foster trust and transparency in decision making. 

  

### 

Be delightful

Encourage bold and creative solutions in the design and implementation of the UX.

  

### 

Be informed

Design with continuous learning from user interactions and behaviors.

  

### 

Be open and connected

Design with interoperability and integration in mind.

  

# 

Applications in the design system

Covalent is the design system for Teradata products. It serves a single purpose of providing a cohesive language for its primary audiences—the designers, content designers, and developers.

These target audiences are masterminds who shape product experiences where their expertise directly impacts the quality of the outcome. To that end, we focus on empowering them with a cohesive language that guides them to build simple and intuitive experiences. Here's how we apply the design principles in the making of the design system:

## 

A point of view

Even though the Covalent components are built on Material Design, we maintain a distinct point of view within Covalent. The color palette, tokens, typography, iconography, and interaction patterns reflect Teradata's brand and our focus on delivering an excellent user experience.

The Covalent documentation uses the Teradata voice. In the documentation, we focus on the user context and articulate the "why," "what," and "how" with product examples.  

## 

For everyone

Inclusivity is imperative for building successful and ethical products that scale. Covalent is in the unique position to champion it as a fundamental standard for design, development, and content. The guidelines for accessibility, inclusive language, and internationalization are built to clearly represent the Teradata users and to be compliant with the industry standards.

## 

Trust

We're building a system that builds trust with the teams. When the system is trusted, adoption comes. We listen to the teams' feedback. To help people learn and apply the guidance, we prioritize a predictable information architecture with simple, clear, and update-to-date documentation. No extra or irrelevant content.

## 

Product-forward

We believe in fostering a robust contribution model. This approach helps us understand the intended growth, usage trajectory, and creation of new use cases. When we stay informed, it helps us make decisions to maintain the system as a reliable go-to-place and to shape the future of Teradata design across the products.
