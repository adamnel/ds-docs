Snackbars are split into two regions, with the message aligned to the left, and the actions (when present) aligned to the right.

1.  Contains the snackbar contents.
    
2.  Text to communicate with the user.
    
3.  (optional) A single regular action is allowed.
    
4.  (optional) A close button.

Animation
