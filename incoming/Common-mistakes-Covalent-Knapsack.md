# Covalent / Common mistakes

Don’t get tripped up on these understandable points of confusion.

# 

Tables should not have margin

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakestables-margincorrect--jLi6ZyC-RY.png)

##### Do

Tables should extend to the edge of the container.

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakestables-marginincorrect--5nTEGWK9mt.png)

##### Don't

Do not include padding around tables.

# 

Inline alerts should not have margin

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakesinline-alert-margincorrect--PQL103dZxn.png)

##### Do

Inline alerts should extend to the edge of the container.

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakesinline-alert-marginincorrect--T4v0wLUiNa.png)

##### Don't

Do not include padding around inline alerts.

# 

Use toggles for instant changes, checkboxes for delayed ones

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakestoggles-checkboxescorrect--1Vv1pXNqJs.png)

##### Do

Use a checkbox if the change doesn’t take effect until you submit the form.

![](https://knapsack.imgix.net/site/covalent/foundationscommon-mistakestoggles-checkboxesincorrect--HSSMgFeJm.png)

##### Don't

A toggle should only be used when the change it triggers takes effect immediately.

To pick up a draggable item, press the space bar. While dragging, use the arrow keys to move the item. Press space again to drop the item in its new position, or press escape to cancel.
