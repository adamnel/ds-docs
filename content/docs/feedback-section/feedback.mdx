Your feedback makes the design system better. Tell us what's working, what's unclear, and where we can improve.

## How to provide feedback

We welcome feedback from all team members. Whether you've found a bug, have a suggestion for improvement, or want to share what's working well, your input helps us build a better design system for everyone.

## What to include

When providing feedback, please include:

* **Context** - What were you trying to accomplish?
* **Details** - What specifically worked well or didn't work?
* **Suggestions** - How do you think it could be improved?
